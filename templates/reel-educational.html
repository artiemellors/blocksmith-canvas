HOW TO PACE
THE 8 STATIONS
01
SKIERG — GO OUT CONTROLLED
Hold 5–8% below race pace for first 500m. The run that follows is where this pays off.
Target: 3:00–3:20 / 1000m
02
SLED PUSH — LEGS, NOT BACK
Low hips, drive through the heel. Back involvement signals you're too heavy or too slow.
03
ROWING — LEGS FIRST
60% legs, 20% core, 20% arms. Rushing the catch kills efficiency.
Target: sub 2:00 / 500m split
04
WALL BALLS — RHYTHM WINS
Set a sustainable cadence from rep 1. Breaking at rep 70 is a disaster. Plan your sets before you start.
