# Blocksmith Canvas — Product Context

## Product Purpose

Static HTML templates for Blocksmith's Instagram channel (@blocksmith_training). Content is injected via URL query params; htmlcsstoimage (hcti) renders each template to a hosted PNG at exact Instagram dimensions. Templates are the visual output of Adsy, Blocksmith's social media agent — not user-facing interfaces.

## Register

Brand. The templates ARE the marketing product. Each rendered frame is Blocksmith's public face to HYROX competitors on Instagram.

## Users

**Primary consumer: HYROX competitors, recreational to Pro.** Time-poor, detail-oriented, allergic to generic fitness content. Scrolling Instagram on a phone — 6–9am commute or 5–10pm evening. Pain: building training plans manually, winging race pacing, no station-specific programming.

**Primary producer: Adsy** (AI agent). Calls hcti to render templates via URL params. No human edits HTML. All variation via params.

## Brand Identity (locked)

- **Fonts**: Bebas Neue (display, all-caps), DM Sans (body/UI), DM Mono (data/metrics)
- **Colour**: Near-black background (#050505), amber accent oklch(60% 0.18 42), destructive oklch(57.7% 0.245 27.3), recovery oklch(50.8% 0.105 166)
- **Bottom bar**: 6px amber on square (1080×1080), 10px amber on reels (1080×1920)
- **Brand wordmark**: Bebas Neue, 22px, 7px letter-spacing, rgba(255,255,255,0.2)

## Tone

Athletic authority, not coaching cheerfulness. Specific over vague. Data-driven. Speak to race-readiness and training discipline. "5 mistakes killing your run split" beats "tips to train better."

## Anti-References

Generic fitness motivational content (Canva templates, pastel fitness posts). GymShark / TikTok fitness aesthetics. Anything that could apply to any sport.

## Canvas Dimensions

- **Single posts / Carousel**: 1080 × 1080px
- **Reels**: 1080 × 1920px
