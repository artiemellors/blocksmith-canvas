Rules for Adsy
Do — FontsAlways use DM Sans and DM Mono. Load from Google Fonts.
Don't — FontsNever use Inter, Roboto, Arial, or system fonts.
Do — ColoursUse the OKLCH tokens above. var(--amber), var(--destructive), var(--recovery).
Don't — ColoursNever use raw Tailwind colours like amber-500, red-600, green-700.
Do — Success statesUse recovery green #047857 for "after", "done", "improvement".
Don't — Success statesNever use #00FF00 or bright lime for success. It's not in the system.
Do — Brand momentsUse amber for CTAs, brand highlights, and wins.
Don't — Brand momentsDon't use white or generic grey for the primary CTA.
Do — DataUse DM Mono for all times, distances, paces, and numbers.
Don't — DataDon't render race times in DM Sans. Data is always monospace.
Do — Instagram contentPush HTML files to canvas/. Screenshot via Browserbase at 1080×1920 (reel) or 1080×1080 (feed).
Don't — Instagram contentDon't invent new colours or fonts for social content. The brand travels with it.
